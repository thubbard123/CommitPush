Deleting a Tab

If you find that you no longer need a tab that you have created, you can delete it at any time.

Deleting a tab is a permanent action. Any items contained in that tab will also be deleted and cannot be undone. Before deleting the , make sure you've selected the correct tab.

To delete a tab, do the following:

  1. Click the tab you want to delete so that it is the active tab.
  2. Right-click the tab. A pop-up menu is displayed.
  3. Choose . The active tab will be deleted.
